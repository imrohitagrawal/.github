# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We are committed to providing a welcoming, inclusive, and harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of background, identity, or experience level.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and constructive in code review, issues, and discussions.
- Critique ideas, not people.
- Assume good intent. Ask for clarification before assuming the worst.
- Accept feedback gracefully.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks of any kind.
- Trolling, insulting comments, or deliberate intimidation.
- Publishing others' private information without permission.
- Any conduct that would be inappropriate in a professional setting.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, issues, and other contributions that do not align with this Code of Conduct. Maintainers will communicate reasons for moderation decisions when appropriate.

## Reporting

Report unacceptable behavior privately to the repository owner. All reports will be handled with discretion.
